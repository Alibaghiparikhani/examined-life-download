Privacy Policy

App: Examined Life: Learn Philosophy

Developer: ABProjects

Overview

Examined Life is an educational philosophy learning app. The app is designed to let users study lessons and answer quizzes without creating an account.

Data collection

This version of the app does not require users to create an account and does not ask users to submit personal information in order to use the course.

Internet access

The course content is bundled inside the app. If future versions add online features, this policy will be updated before those features are released.

Children

The app is intended as general educational content. It is not designed to collect personal information from children.

Contact

For questions about this app or privacy policy, contact ABProjects through the official project page.
