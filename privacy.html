Privacy Policy

App: Examined Life: Philosophy

Developer: ABProjects

Overview

Examined Life: Philosophy is an educational philosophy learning app. It provides offline study pages, lessons, quizzes, and level reviews.

Data collection

This version of the app does not require users to create an account and does not ask users to submit personal information.

Offline course content

The main course content is bundled inside the app. Users can study the lessons and answer quizzes without sending study data to a server.

Ads and tracking

This version of the app does not contain ads and is not designed to track users for advertising purposes.

Changes

If future versions add online features, analytics, accounts, or other data-related features, this privacy policy will be updated.

Contact

For questions about this app or privacy policy, contact ABProjects through the official project page.
