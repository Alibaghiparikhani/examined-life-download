What the app does

Examined Life is a mobile learning app for philosophy. It guides learners through a full crash course using short study lessons, quizzes, and level assessments.

Structured course

Follow a complete philosophy path from basic questions to ethics, knowledge, politics, art, religion, global traditions, and real-life philosophy.

Lessons and quizzes

Each lesson includes study content and quiz practice. Level gates review larger sections of the course.

Multilingual content

The app includes English, Turkish, and German learner-facing content.

Offline-first

The course content is bundled in the app. No account is required to study the lessons.

How to install on Android

  1. Tap Download for Android.
  2. When the APK finishes downloading, open it.
  3. If Android asks, allow installation from your browser or file manager.
  4. Tap Install.
  5. Open Examined Life from your app launcher.

Android may show a warning because this APK is installed outside Google Play. Only install the app if you downloaded it from this official ABProjects page.
