How to install on Android

  1. Tap Download APK.
  2. Open the downloaded APK file.
  3. If Android asks, allow installation from your browser or file manager.
  4. Tap Install.
  5. Open Examined Life from your app launcher.

Android may show a warning because this is a direct APK download instead of a Google Play install. That is normal for apps installed outside the Play Store.

What is this app?

Examined Life is a philosophy learning app. It is basically a complete offline course: a collection of study slides, lesson pages, quizzes, and level review gates bundled inside one Android app.

The course starts from simple questions like “What is philosophy?” and then moves through logic, ancient philosophy, ethics, knowledge, reality, politics, religion, meaning, art, modern philosophy, global traditions, technology, work, love, happiness, and the examined life.

You do not need an account to use it. The lessons and quizzes are stored inside the app, so there is no need to upload your files, send personal study data, or connect to a server just to read the course.
